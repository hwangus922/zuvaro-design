Privacy Policy

Zuvaro is a social dare app. This policy describes what we collect and why.

Information we collect

  • Account info (email, display name, handle, avatar emoji)
  • Photo proof you upload when completing dares
  • Group chat messages and dare activity within your crew
  • Usage data needed to run leaderboards, notifications, and quest progress

How we use it

We use your data to operate Zuvaro: authenticate you, store submissions, award points after review, and show activity to friends in your group.

Photo proof & moderation

Proof photos are reviewed by moderators before points are awarded. Rejected submissions may be deleted per our retention rules.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Friends in your group can see your profile, points, and approved activity.

Questions: support@zuvaro.app
