Privacy Policy

Zuvaro (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Zuvaro mobile app and website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Zuvaro, you agree to this policy.

Who we are

Zuvaro is a social dare app where friends complete challenges, submit photo proof, and compete on leaderboards. Contact: support@zuvaro.app

Eligibility

Zuvaro is intended for users 17 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, username (handle), avatar emoji, and authentication identifiers from Sign in with Apple or email sign-up.
  • Profile & gameplay data: points, streaks, quest progress, region selection, crew membership, and leaderboard rankings.
  • User-generated content: photo proof submissions, optional captions, group chat messages, and custom dares you create.
  • Safety data: block lists, support reports, and moderation records when you report content or users.
  • Device permissions: photo library access when you choose a proof photo (we do not access your library without your action).

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

How we use your information

  • Create and secure your account
  • Operate dares, proof upload, points, and leaderboards
  • Display your activity to members of your crew and on leaderboards you participate in
  • Review photo proof before awarding points (human moderation)
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Send in-app notifications about proof status, dares, and leaderboard activity

Photo proof & moderation

When you submit photo proof, the image is stored securely and reviewed by a human moderator before points are awarded. Rejected submissions may be deleted. Moderators can view your submission, caption, and profile information associated with the submission.

How we share information

  • With your crew: friends in your group can see your display name, username, avatar, messages, dares, and approved activity.
  • On leaderboards: regional and global boards show your username, avatar, and points when you participate.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase for authentication, database, and file storage. Data is processed under their security and privacy terms.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect users and the platform.

Data retention

  • Account data is kept while your account is active.
  • When you delete your account in the app, we delete your authentication record, profile, messages, submissions, stored photos, and related personal data promptly.
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics and abuse prevention.

Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account in the app. Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, proof photos, chat messages, and personal data from our systems, subject to brief backup retention required for security and legal compliance.

Your choices & rights

  • Edit your display name and username in Settings
  • Block users from chat and manage blocked users in Settings
  • Report inappropriate content or users from chat
  • Contact us to access, correct, or delete your data: support@zuvaro.app

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws. We will honor valid requests within applicable timeframes.

Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level database security, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; use Zuvaro at your own risk for sensitive content.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We take steps to protect your information consistent with this policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests: support@zuvaro.app
