Privacy Policy

Zuvaro (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the Zuvaro mobile app and website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. By using Zuvaro, you agree to this policy and our Terms of Service.

Who we are

Zuvaro is a social dare app where friends complete missions, submit photo proof, and compete on leaderboards. Contact: support@zuvaro.app

Eligibility

Zuvaro is intended for users 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are under 18, you should use Zuvaro only with permission from a parent or legal guardian where required by law.

If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, contact us at support@zuvaro.app and we will delete it.

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, optional phone number (if you verify it), display name, username (handle), avatar emoji, and authentication identifiers from Sign in with Apple or email sign-up.
  • Contacts (optional): if you use Find Friends, phone numbers from your address book are read on your device and sent to our servers only to match against other Zuvaro users who opted in to discovery. We do not upload or store your full contact list or contact names.
  • SMS: if you verify your phone and enable text updates, we store your number and may send transactional texts (verification codes, proof results, crew activity). Message and data rates may apply.
  • Profile & gameplay data: points, streaks, quest progress, region selection, crew membership, leaderboard rankings, and referral counts.
  • User-generated content: photo proof submissions, optional captions, group chat messages, and custom dares you create.
  • Safety data: block lists, support reports, and moderation records when you report content or users.
  • Usage analytics (optional): app screens viewed, buttons tapped, challenge flows completed, and similar product-interaction events. When you are signed in, these events may be linked to your account ID. We do not include dare text, photos, or chat message content in analytics events.
  • Device permissions: photo library access when you choose a proof photo (we do not access your library without your action).
  • Technical data: standard app and server logs needed for security, debugging, and fraud prevention.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

How we use your information

  • Create and secure your account
  • Operate dares, proof upload, points, leaderboards, and prize pools
  • Display your activity to members of your crew and on leaderboards you participate in
  • Review photo proof before awarding points (human moderation)
  • Respond to support requests and enforce our Terms of Service
  • Send in-app notifications about proof status, dares, and leaderboard activity
  • Measure app performance and improve features through optional usage analytics

Analytics choices

Usage analytics are enabled by default. You can turn them off at any time in the app under Settings → Privacy preferences → Share usage analytics. When disabled, we stop recording new analytics events from your device, except a one-time preference-change event needed to honor your choice.

Photo proof & moderation

When you submit photo proof, the image is stored securely and reviewed by a human moderator before points are awarded. Rejected submissions may be deleted. Moderators can view your submission, caption, and profile information associated with the submission.

How we share information

  • With your crew: members of your group can see your display name, username, avatar, messages, dares, and approved activity.
  • On leaderboards: regional and global boards show your username, avatar, and points when you participate.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase for authentication, database, file storage, and analytics event storage. Data is processed under their security and privacy terms.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law, to respond to lawful requests, or to protect users and the platform.

Data retention

  • Account data is kept while your account is active.
  • When you delete your account in the app, we delete your authentication record, profile, messages, submissions, stored photos, and related personal data promptly.
  • Usage analytics events linked to your account are deleted or anonymized when your account is deleted.
  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot reasonably identify you may be retained for analytics, security, and abuse prevention.

Account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Profile → Delete Account in the app. Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, proof photos, chat messages, and personal data from our systems, subject to brief backup retention required for security and legal compliance.

Your choices & rights

  • Edit your display name and username in Settings
  • Disable usage analytics in Settings → Privacy preferences
  • Manage phone verification, friend discovery, and SMS toggles in Settings → Phone & SMS
  • Remove your phone number from Settings → Phone & SMS
  • Block users from chat and manage blocked users in Settings
  • Report inappropriate content or users from chat
  • Contact us to access, correct, or delete your data: support@zuvaro.app

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under GDPR, CCPA, or similar laws. We will honor valid requests within applicable timeframes.

Security

We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level database security, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; do not submit sensitive personal information in dares, captions, or chat messages.

International transfers

Your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We take steps to protect your information consistent with this policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised version on this page and update the “Last updated” date. Continued use after changes become effective means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests: support@zuvaro.app
